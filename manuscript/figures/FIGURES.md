**`fp_reduction` inputs:** DP3 table `metrics_native_cyl.csv` (cols `af3_n_clash_res`, `cylinder_ca_clashes`, `cylinder_native_aware`), produced on Gemini by `scripts/add_native_cylinder.py --exclude_dist 1.0`. Verified result: among 3,186 clash-free designs, heavily-penalized (cylinder ≥ 10) dropped 1,112 → 384 (728 false positives removed). NB: all cylinder figures (`fp_reduction`, `fp_selectivity`, `passes_overlay`, and the cylinder columns of `dp3_metric_binding`) depend on the cylinder geometry (offset/R/H/carve), which is being validated by a ground-truth sweep — see `docs/CYLINDER_PARAMS.md`; if the geometry changes, these regenerate.

**`dp3_binding_scatter.png`** — per-antibody log10(1+NoAb) vs log10(1+Ab), cognate scaffolded designs in red, for the 8 usable DP3 mAbs. Inputs: `data/dp3_binding/` (two raw assay CSVs, IM226 + IM229) joined to `dp2.parquet` metrics by `scripts/build_dp3_binding_join.py` → `results/dp3_binding_metrics.csv`. `analyze_dp3_binding.py` prints every number `sec:experimental` cites (403/403 join, all-passing `is_pass`, metric ranges, per-Ab counts/enrichments, pooled rho −0.43 vs within-Ab collapse). Provenance + the all-passing reliability caveat in `data/dp3_binding/README.md`.

**`dp3_metric_binding.png`** — 2×5 grid: each filter metric + plain/native-aware cylinder vs cognate log-enrichment, pooled (top) and within-antibody fixed-effect (bottom). Inputs: `results/dp3_binding_metrics.csv` + `results/assayed_native_cyl.csv` (cylinder on the assayed structures, from `scripts/assayed_native_cylinder.py` on Gemini). Verified within-Ab Pearson: cylinder native-aware −0.22 (best, survives deconfounding), plain −0.18, epitope RMSD −0.14, overall RMSD −0.08, PAE +0.07.

**`island_split_metrics.png`** — DP3 (RFD1+MPNN, `dp2.parquet`) four-filter metric distributions split by epitope island count (`epitope_chunks` 1 vs 2), density-normalized (N is uneven: 549 single-island vs 123,813 two-island designs with an AF3 result). Single-island designs sit at better values on every metric (median epitope RMSD 2.22 vs 3.86 Å, mean PAE 9.5 vs 11.9), yet the binary four-filter pass rate runs the other way (per-total 0.24% vs 0.56%; per-valid 0.36% vs 0.68%) because 779 of the 838 two-island passes come from two outlier epitopes (`7ox3_0P` 630, `5fhx_1P` 149); strip the outlier and two-island falls to 0.17% per-valid, below single-island. The script prints both per-total and per-valid rates plus the outlier breakdown. Source is the external `dp2.parquet` (see `data/README.md`).

From `dp2.parquet`, the 46 `epitope_chunks==2` mAb epitopes; an *island* = one contig `A`-segment, *island size* = its residue span. Verified counts: 46 epitopes, 92 islands, **87 scaffolded** (size≥2) → 87 targets → 435 designs at top-5/island; **5 size-1 islands skipped** (one each in `2h32_0P`, `3q1s_0P`, `6o9i_0P`, `7a3t_0P`, `7tzh_0P`). Build the PDF with `tectonic main.tex` (not latexmk in this env).

**C5 titration coverage** — five panels, one per sampled axis (epitope RMSD, epitope PAE, overall RMSD, **AF3 clash (real)**, native-aware cylinder): full pool (grey) vs the C5 farthest-point sample (blue, n=3,000).
