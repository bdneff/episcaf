\begin{abstract}
We scaffold target epitopes onto de novo proteins and validate them with AlphaFold3.
For antigens with a known antibody (the ``DP3'' set) we score designs against a
four-filter ground truth; for antigens without an antibody (a tiled 12-mer set) we
replace the antibody-clash term with a native-antigen-aware cylinder surrogate that
measures whether scaffold mass intrudes into the space an antibody would need. A single
config-driven composite scorer ranks designs in both settings. This document is the
living record of the project: every number and figure is regenerated by a named script
from \texttt{results/}.
\end{abstract}
